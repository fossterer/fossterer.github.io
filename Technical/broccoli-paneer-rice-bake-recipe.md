Here is an Indian-inspired vegetarian oven meals for two adults, each with broccoli and rice, in the 500-600 calorie range per serving. It offers balanced protein and micronutrients, using common pantry staples for 

## Ingredients (for 2 servings)
*1.5 cups cooked rice (brown or basmati)
* 250g broccoli florets (fresh or frozen)
* 100g paneer, diced
* 1 small onion (sliced)
* 1 tsp oil (for sautéing)
* 2 tbsp low-fat Greek yogurt
* Spices: cumin, turmeric, black pepper, salt, chili powder
* 1 tbsp shredded cheese, fresh cilantro for garnish

## Steps
Steam or blanch broccoli till just tender.
Sauté onion lightly in 1 tsp oil with cumin and turmeric
Toss rice, broccoli, paneer, sautéed onion, yogurt, and spices in a bowl.
Spread evenly in an oven-safe dish, top with a sprinkling of shredded cheese.
Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 20–25 minutes, till warmed through and lightly golden on top.
Finish with cilantro.

## Nutrition per serving
~520 kcal, 22g protein, 12g fat, 75g carbs (adjust paneer quantity for macros)

## Estimated Prep and Cook Times
* Prep Time - 10 min
* Oven Time - 20–25 min
* **Total Time - 30–35 min**
