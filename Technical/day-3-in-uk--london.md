# Day 3 in UK - London

* paying in cash over card saves you - at least in small takeaway shops. Same as in some US businesses.
* "Takeout" in US = "Takeaway" in UK
* UK is a "political entity" comprised of 4 "countries": England (has London), Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland. Full name is "The United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland"
* Great Britain is the "geographic term" that excludes "Northern Ireland"
