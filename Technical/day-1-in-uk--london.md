# Day 1 in UK - London

* *Tube* is underground rail that goes just inside city. *Rail/Train* is countrywide network.
* City public transport is divided into zones. 6 Zones are 6 concentric circles
* Zone 1 is the central London. If you stay in Zone 2 and all your designations are in Zone 1, which is true for most tourist spots, a day pass is no better than 'Pay as you go with contactless/Tap'. Just mind the foreign transaction fee of your card provider 
* But if you are crossing more than 1 zone, a day pass or even a weekly pass can save you. Download 'TFL Go' app. That's 'Transportation For London's
* On streets, signs are more at eye level compared to those in US
